Statistical Concepts

Here's a brief explanation of the statistical concepts used in player analysis:

Z-score

A z-score tells you how many standard deviations a data point is from the mean. The formula is:

z = (x - μ) / σ

Where:

  • x: The data point
  • μ: The mean
  • σ: The standard deviation

Standard Deviation

Standard deviation measures the spread of data. The sample standard deviation formula is:

s = sqrt[ Σ(xi - x̄)² / (n - 1) ]

Where:

  • xi: Each data point
  • x̄: The sample mean
  • n: The number of data points
